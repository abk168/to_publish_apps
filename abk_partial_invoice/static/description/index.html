Custom Fixed Amount Partial Invoice

This module allows users to generate partial invoices from Sales Orders using custom fixed amounts and percentages.

📧 Email info@aboutknowledge.com 🌐 Website https://www.aboutknowledge.com

Key Highlights

💰 Flexible Partial Invoicing

Generate partial invoices from Sales Orders using either a custom fixed amount or a percentage of the total order value.

📦 Create Invoice from Delivery

Easily create invoices directly from Delivery Orders (DN), ensuring accurate invoicing based on delivered quantities.

🔀 Multiple Delivery Orders

Supports multiple Delivery Notes (DNs) for a single Sale Order, enabling flexible delivery and invoicing workflows.

📊 Tracking & Visibility

Track deposited, invoiced, and remaining percentages directly from the Sale Order for better financial control.

🧙 Wizard-Based Flow

User-friendly wizard to generate invoices using fixed amount or percentage-based down payments in just a few clicks.

🧾 Invoice & Delivery Sync

Automatically keeps invoices and delivery orders synchronized to prevent over-invoicing or quantity mismatches.

🧩 Odoo 17 Ready

Fully compatible with Odoo 17 Enterprise edition, following standard accounting and invoicing practices.

📸 Screenshots

Step 1: Open the Sale Order and confirm it.
Step 2: Click Create Invoice and choose Down Payment (Fixed Amount or Percentage). Enter the fixed amount or percentage and confirm.
Step 3: Click Create Draft Invoice. The system creates a draft invoice with the selected custom amount or percentage.
📦 Create Invoice from Delivery Order (DN)
Step 4:click Create Invoice, Open the related Delivery Order (DN), select DN, and confirm.
Step 5: A draft invoice is created from the Delivery Order. Review and validate the invoice.
